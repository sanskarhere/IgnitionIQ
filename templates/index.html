Algerian FWI Predictor🔥🌲

🌍 What This Website Does?

This tool predicts the Fire Weather Index (FWI) for two major regions of Algeria using real-time environmental data. FWI is a global standard used to estimate forest fire risk based on weather conditions.

📊 What Influences FWI?

Factor Effect on FWI Description
🌡️ Temperature Higher temp → Higher FWI Heat dries fuel, increasing ignition potential
💧 Humidity (RH) Lower RH → Higher FWI Dry air reduces moisture in vegetation
🌬️ Wind Speed (Ws) Higher wind → Higher FWI Wind accelerates fire spread and intensity
🌧️ Rainfall More rain → Lower FWI Rain increases fuel moisture, lowering fire risk

🗺️ Regional Differences

Region 0: Fires often occur during dry spells with moderate wind and high temperature.
Region 1: Fires are more intense and frequent during heatwaves with low humidity and strong wind.

🧠 How Prediction Works

We use machine learning models trained on historical Algerian forest data to predict FWI values based on daily weather inputs. These predictions help assess fire risk and guide preventive action.

🚨 Why It Matters

FWI-based forecasting helps forest officials and citizens prepare for fire-prone conditions before they escalate. It’s a proactive step toward protecting ecosystems, wildlife, and human lives.
